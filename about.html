About Niyantri Café

Niyantri Café draws inspiration from urban jungle aesthetics—combining biophilic design with a warm, elegant atmosphere. Our Nadargul location blends greenery, natural textures, and thoughtful detailing to create a serene daily sanctuary for guests, students, and remote workers alike.

Walls mix bright white with a moody botanical feature wall. A high ceiling hosts a living canopy of vines, while the coffee bar showcases emerald-tiled fronts and brass glass shelves. Expect lush seating, artisan ceramics, and a community bookshelf that invites you to linger.
